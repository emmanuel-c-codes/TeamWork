Frequently Asked Questions

Is EcoMonitor free to use?

Yes, EcoMonitor is a community-driven initiative and completely free for every Nigerian citizen to use and report issues.

How are reports handled?

Once an issue is reported, it is geotagged and added to our real-time database, making it visible to community leaders and relevant local agencies.

Can I report anonymously?

Yes, we prioritize community safety. You can submit reports without revealing your personal identity.
